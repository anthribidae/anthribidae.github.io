# About Anthribidae of the World

This is a website developed by [Samanta Orellana](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-4098-5823), currently a Postdoc in the Franz Lab of Weevil Systematics and Evolution at the University of Kansas (KU). The site is designed to display the diversity of the group across the world, featuring specimens available in the collections that have kindly provided them: **Arizona State University Biocollections** (ASUCOB, ASUHIC, Dr. Sangmi Lee), University of **Kansas Natural History Museum** ([KUNHM](https://biodiversity.ku.edu/entomology/collection), Dr. Zachary Falin), **American Museum of Natural History** ([AMNH](https://www.amnh.org/research/invertebrate-zoology/collections), Dr. Lee Herman), **New Zealand Arthropod Collection** ([NZAC](https://www.landcareresearch.co.nz/tools-and-resources/collections/new-zealand-arthropod-collection-nzac/), Dr. Richard Leschen, Grace Hall).
